Gas Furnace Installation Services

Gas furnaces remain the most popular heating choice for Ellisville homes due to their efficiency, reliability, and operating cost advantages. Our gas furnace installation services include comprehensive evaluation of your home's gas supply and venting requirements, proper sizing calculations to ensure optimal performance, installation of all necessary gas lines and connections, and thorough testing to ensure safe and efficient operation.

Modern gas furnaces achieve Annual Fuel Utilization Efficiency (AFUE) ratings of 80% to 98%, representing significant efficiency improvements over older systems. High-efficiency condensing furnaces can achieve AFUE ratings of 90% or higher, providing substantial energy savings and reduced heating costs for Ellisville homeowners.

Our gas furnace installation process includes evaluation of existing gas line capacity and connections, installation or upgrading of gas supply lines as needed, proper venting system installation or modification, integration with existing ductwork and electrical systems, comprehensive testing for gas leaks and proper combustion, and customer education on safe operation and maintenance.

Electric Furnace Installation Solutions

Electric furnaces provide an excellent heating solution for homes without natural gas service or where gas installation is not practical. Our electric furnace installation services ensure proper electrical connections, adequate power supply, and optimal system configuration for efficient heating performance.

Electric furnaces offer several advantages including clean operation with no combustion byproducts, quiet operation with minimal noise, compact design that requires less space, no venting requirements, and excellent safety record with no risk of gas leaks or carbon monoxide. These features make electric furnaces an attractive option for many Ellisville homes.

Our electric furnace installation includes evaluation of electrical service capacity and requirements, installation or upgrading of electrical connections as needed, proper integration with existing ductwork, installation of appropriate electrical disconnect and safety devices, and comprehensive testing to ensure safe and efficient operation.

Modern electric furnaces can achieve 95-100% efficiency in converting electrical energy to heat, and when paired with programmable or smart thermostats, they can provide excellent comfort control and energy management for Ellisville homes.

High-Efficiency Furnace Systems

High-efficiency furnaces represent the latest in heating technology, offering exceptional energy savings and environmental benefits. Our high-efficiency furnace installation services help Ellisville homeowners take advantage of the most advanced heating technology available while maximizing energy savings and comfort.

Condensing furnaces achieve high efficiency by capturing and utilizing heat from combustion gases that would otherwise be vented outside. This process requires special venting materials and drainage systems, which our expert technicians install according to manufacturer specifications and local building codes.

Variable-speed blower motors in high-efficiency furnaces provide improved comfort control by adjusting airflow to match heating demands. These systems operate more quietly and efficiently while providing better humidity control and air circulation throughout your Ellisville home.

Two-stage and modulating gas valves allow high-efficiency furnaces to adjust heat output based on actual heating needs, providing more consistent temperatures and improved efficiency compared to single-stage systems. Our installation ensures proper setup and calibration of these advanced features.

Proper Sizing and Load Calculations

Proper furnace sizing is critical for optimal performance, efficiency, and comfort. Our comprehensive load calculation process ensures that your new furnace is properly sized for your Ellisville home's specific heating requirements, taking into account factors such as home size, insulation levels, window efficiency, air infiltration, and local climate conditions.

Oversized furnaces waste energy, cycle on and off frequently, and provide poor humidity control and comfort. Undersized furnaces struggle to maintain comfortable temperatures and operate continuously during cold weather, leading to higher energy costs and reduced equipment life.

Our Manual J load calculation process follows Air Conditioning Contractors of America (ACCA) standards to determine precise heating requirements for your home. This scientific approach ensures optimal system sizing and performance while avoiding the problems associated with improperly sized equipment.

Factors considered in our load calculations include building envelope characteristics, insulation levels and types, window and door specifications, air infiltration rates, internal heat gains, and local weather data. This comprehensive analysis ensures that your new furnace provides optimal comfort and efficiency.

Ductwork Evaluation and Modifications

Existing ductwork must be properly evaluated and potentially modified to ensure optimal performance from your new furnace. Our ductwork evaluation services identify any issues that could affect system performance and recommend necessary modifications or improvements.

Common ductwork issues that affect furnace performance include undersized or oversized ducts, poor duct design and layout, air leaks and inadequate sealing, insufficient insulation, and blocked or restricted airflow. Our duct cleaning services can address contamination issues while our installation team handles structural modifications.

Ductwork modifications may include resizing supply and return ducts, sealing air leaks and improving connections, adding or improving duct insulation, installing dampers for zone control, and improving return air pathways. These modifications ensure that your new furnace operates at peak efficiency.

Venting and Exhaust Systems

Proper venting is essential for safe and efficient furnace operation, particularly for gas furnaces that produce combustion byproducts. Our venting installation services ensure compliance with all safety codes and manufacturer requirements while optimizing system performance.

High-efficiency condensing furnaces require special PVC or other approved plastic venting materials due to the lower temperature and corrosive nature of exhaust gases. Our technicians are experienced in installing these specialized venting systems according to manufacturer specifications.

Venting installation includes proper sizing of vent pipes, correct slope for condensate drainage, appropriate termination locations and clearances, and integration with existing chimney or vent systems where applicable. We ensure that all venting meets local building codes and safety requirements.
