Comprehensive Home Energy Audits

Professional home energy audits provide the foundation for effective efficiency improvements by identifying specific areas where energy is being wasted and prioritizing upgrades based on cost-effectiveness and impact. Our comprehensive energy audit services use advanced diagnostic equipment and proven assessment techniques to provide detailed analysis of your home's energy performance.

Our energy audit process includes blower door testing to measure air leakage and identify infiltration sources, thermal imaging to detect insulation gaps and thermal bridging, duct blaster testing to evaluate ductwork performance and leakage, combustion safety testing for gas appliances, detailed inspection of insulation levels and building envelope performance, and analysis of HVAC equipment efficiency and operation.

Advanced diagnostic equipment includes infrared cameras that reveal heat loss and air leakage patterns, digital manometers for pressure testing and airflow measurement, combustion analyzers for safety and efficiency testing, and moisture meters for identifying potential problems that could affect efficiency and indoor air quality.

Following the audit, we provide detailed reports with prioritized recommendations, cost estimates for suggested improvements, projected energy savings and payback periods, and information about available rebates and financing options. This comprehensive analysis helps you make informed decisions about which upgrades will provide the greatest benefits for your investment.

Insulation Upgrades and Improvements

Proper insulation is crucial for maintaining comfortable indoor temperatures while minimizing heating and cooling costs. Our insulation upgrade services address all areas of your home where inadequate insulation may be contributing to energy waste, providing solutions that deliver immediate comfort improvements and long-term energy savings.

Insulation upgrade areas include attic insulation to prevent heat loss through the roof, wall insulation for existing homes with inadequate thermal protection, basement and crawl space insulation to address foundation heat loss, and specialized insulation for unique areas such as bonus rooms, additions, and difficult-to-reach spaces.

Insulation types and applications include blown-in cellulose or fiberglass for attic applications, spray foam insulation for air sealing and high-performance applications, batt insulation for standard wall and floor applications, and rigid foam insulation for basement walls and specialty applications. Each type offers specific advantages for different applications and performance requirements.

Our insulation services include assessment of existing insulation levels and condition, removal of old or damaged insulation when necessary, air sealing to address gaps and penetrations before insulation installation, professional installation using proper techniques and safety procedures, and quality verification to ensure optimal performance and coverage.

Air Sealing and Weatherization Services

Air sealing is one of the most cost-effective energy efficiency improvements, addressing the gaps and cracks that allow conditioned air to escape and outside air to infiltrate your home. Our comprehensive air sealing services identify and seal air leakage sources throughout your home, providing immediate improvements in comfort and efficiency.

Common air leakage sources include gaps around windows and doors, penetrations for plumbing, electrical, and HVAC systems, attic access points and recessed lighting, basement rim joists and foundation connections, and ductwork connections and joints. Addressing these leakage sources can significantly reduce energy waste while improving indoor comfort.

Air sealing materials and techniques include caulking and weatherstripping for windows and doors, spray foam for larger gaps and irregular spaces, gaskets and seals for electrical outlets and fixtures, and specialized sealants for ductwork and HVAC penetrations. Each application requires appropriate materials and techniques for effective, long-lasting results.

Professional air sealing provides benefits including reduced heating and cooling costs, improved indoor comfort with fewer drafts and temperature variations, better indoor air quality through controlled ventilation, and enhanced HVAC system performance through reduced load and improved efficiency.
